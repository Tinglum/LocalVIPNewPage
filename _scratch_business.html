Frequently Asked Question

What Exactly Does LocalVIP Do?

LocalVIP brings you new customers. You offer a deal, we do the marketing, and you only pay 10% after the customer pays their bill. It’s risk-free and designed to fill your calendar, especially on slower days.

How Is This Different From Groupon or Other Marketing Apps?

We don’t charge you upfront. There are no contracts, no credit card fees, and no complicated dashboards.  LocalVIP only gets paid after you do, and our charge is only 10% of which 20% goes diretly to local schools or charities.

Will This Be Hard To Set Up Or Manage?

Nope. We help you set your offer, launch your listing, and start promoting right away. You can be live in under 24 hours, and we handle the rest.

How Will I Know If It’s Working?

You’ll see each customer tracked in real time — including what they bought and how much you owe. It’s fully transparent and always live.

Is This Just Another Discount App?

Not at all. It’s a marketing engine that brings you paying, high-intent customers — not bargain hunters. And you keep 90% of the sale.

What Kind of Offers Work Best?

Simple, strong, and time-sensitive offers perform best. Think “20% off your tab,” “10% cash-back”, “two for one,” or “$10 off lunch.” We’ll help you craft the right one.

How to get started with LocalVIP in your location?

Everything starts with Community Giveback Week: the official launch event for LocalVIP in your city.

This is a one-week campaign where at least 100 top-rated local businesses come together to offer great deals to their community. It’s designed to create a surge of foot traffic, media buzz, and local goodwill, all while raising money for neighborhood schools. These Local schools will rally their supporters, and we bring the marketing muscle to drive consumers into participating businesses like yours.

It’s the perfect time to get discovered by new customers, build loyalty fast, and show your support for the causes your customers care about.

Once Community Giveback Week ends, you shift into the standard LocalVIP marketing schedule, which is designed to keep driving traffic your way — on the days you need it most.

What happens after Community Giveback Week?

Once community giveback week is over, you choose a “LocalVIP boost day” — typically your slowest day of the week. Our system is built to move get you your customers in the slower times of the week — helping you smooth out your traffic, and make better use of your staff and space.

And the best part? There are no upfront costs, no subscriptions, and no guesswork. You only pay 10% after a successful transaction — and 2% of that goes straight to local schools and nonprofits, so your participation keeps giving back.
