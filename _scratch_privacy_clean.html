5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

To provide an efficient, responsive, and personalized experience, LocalVIP uses cookies and similar tracking technologies in both our mobile application and web-based services. These tools are essential for maintaining security, analyzing performance, and tailoring interactions based on user behavior. This section explains what technologies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

5.1 What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by our systems or by third-party partners when you use our Services. In addition to cookies, we may also use:

  • Pixel tags (also known as web beacons): These are small graphics that monitor user interactions with content and can measure the effectiveness of email campaigns or user engagement.
  • Local storage and in-app data caching: These methods store information within your device to speed up loading times and maintain session consistency.
  • Device and session identifiers: These help us distinguish repeat visits, track app activity, and detect abnormal use or potential abuse.

These technologies do not give us access to your device, files, or personal content beyond what you explicitly share or permit within the app or browser.

5.2 Why We Use These Technologies

We use cookies and tracking tools for several essential purposes:

  • Performance Optimization: To load pages and app features more quickly and improve responsiveness across device types and network conditions.
  • Functionality: To remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, and support navigation between sections of the app without disruptions.
  • Analytics and Diagnostics: To gather data on how features are used, which screens are most visited, and where users may encounter bugs or slowdowns. This helps us make better design and engineering decisions.
  • Security Monitoring: To detect suspicious patterns, such as repeated failed login attempts or automated scanning of QR codes, helping to protect against fraud or abuse.
  • Location-Dependent Personalization: If location permissions are granted, we may use tracking to offer geographically relevant rewards, notifications, or reminders.

We do not use tracking technologies to collect sensitive personal data or sell information to third parties. All tracking is used for internal operational and service-related purposes only.

5.3 Third-Party Services and Analytics

We may incorporate third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics for Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar tools to help measure usage patterns, campaign effectiveness, and system performance. These tools may use cookies or SDKs within the app to collect usage data in a way that does not identify you personally.

All third-party services used are contractually obligated to adhere to privacy standards aligned with U.S. data protection requirements and must not use your data for unrelated purposes.

5.4 Managing Cookie and Tracking Preferences

As a user, you have the ability to control how and whether tracking technologies are used through the following means:

  • Browser settings: You can adjust cookie settings in your web browser to block or delete cookies. Each browser provides different tools, and changing settings may impact your user experience.
  • Device-level permissions: On mobile devices, you can disable app permissions related to location or data usage through your operating system settings.
  • In-app preferences: Where available, we may offer toggle options within your user profile or privacy settings to adjust notification and tracking behavior.
  • Opt-out mechanisms: For analytics services that support opt-out functionality, we honor industry-standard protocols where applicable.

Note that disabling certain cookies or tracking tools may affect the functionality or personalization capabilities of LocalVIP, including how offers and deals are delivered to you.

5.5 App Tracking Transparency and Tracking

LocalVIP does not track you as Apple defines tracking, meaning we do not link data collected from this app with data collected from other companies’ apps or websites for targeted advertising or advertising measurement, and we do not share user or device data with data brokers for those purposes.

6.4 User Control and Opt-Out

You are always in control of whether or not LocalVIP can access your location:

  • You may adjust your settings to allow only “while using the app” access or to block background tracking entirely.
  • You may still use the app without enabling location data, but some features—like local deals, in-store QR scans, or proximity alerts—may be limited or unavailable.

We respect your choices. No location-based features will be activated unless permission is granted. If at any point you wish to revoke location access or delete any stored location data, please contact us at support@localvip.com, and we will comply promptly.

  • The right to obtain a copy of your data in a portable format.

10.4 Virginia (Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act – VCDPA)

Virginia residents are granted rights to:

  • Access and confirm what personal data we collect about them.
  • Correct inaccuracies in personal data.
  • Delete personal data provided or obtained.
