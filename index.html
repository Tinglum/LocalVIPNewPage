About

We Are LocalVIP

We believe every local purchase should mean more. LocalVIP is a free mobile app that helps you save money at top-rated businesses, support the causes you care about, and earn bonus rewards for sharing with friends.

Launched by a small team of entrepreneurs and community leaders, LocalVIP connects consumers, businesses, schools, and charities in one powerful loop. It’s more than just cashback — it’s a movement to grow local economies through everyday spending.

From coffee shops to gyms, LocalVIP helps local heroes like you make an impact, one transaction at a time.

📱 Download the app and start making a difference today:

the mission

To make everyday spending meaningful. LocalVIP empowers individuals to support small businesses and local fundraising through simple, everyday actions. By using the app to pay for meals, massages, fitness classes or haircuts, users automatically direct a portion of every transaction to schools and charities they choose.

We’re on a mission to put purpose behind every purchase — helping communities grow stronger through generosity and connection.

The Vision

We envision a world where supporting your favorite local restaurant also means funding a classroom. Where shopping locally becomes the most rewarding way to give back. And where everyday people — not just big brands — drive change by using a smarter, more generous payment platform.

Our goal is to make LocalVIP available in every city, empowering millions of users to do good while living their lives.

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you interact with LocalVIP, you may choose to provide personal information directly to us. This includes:

  • Registration Data: To create a Hero account, you are required to provide your first name, last name, city of residence, email address, and password. These details are necessary to verify your identity, maintain your referral lineage, and personalize your experience.
  • Business Data: Businesses registering with LocalVIP must provide the business name, the name and contact details of a representative, category or industry classification, business address, and other campaign-related preferences. This information supports offer creation, payout eligibility, and public visibility within the app.

We use this data to generate your account profile, track participation in campaigns, and deliver relevant offers.

2.2 Referral Relationship Data

LocalVIP is built on a multi-level referral system. To support this structure:

  • We track who referred you and who you refer, up to 10 levels deep.
  • Heroes can view the first name, last initial, and city of users connected to them within this tree.
  • Businesses can view only the business name of others within their referral structure—never personal contact data.

This system ensures transparency and accountability while maintaining strict limits on data visibility.

2.3 Interaction and Activity Data

We collect data as you engage with our Services:

  • QR Code & Link Interactions: When you scan a QR code or click a link to sign up, redeem, or refer someone, we log the event, timestamp, and context to confirm referral accuracy and reward eligibility.
  • App Usage & Behavior: We monitor how you interact with the app, including screens visited, time spent, clicks, feature engagement, and redemption history. This helps us improve app usability and offer performance.

2.4 Device and Technical Information

When you use LocalVIP, we may automatically collect technical information such as IP address, device model, operating system, app version, browser type, language settings, and device identifiers. We also collect diagnostics and crash data, such as crash logs, performance data, and error reports, to help identify and fix bugs, improve stability, and maintain secure operation of the Services.

This information is vital for platform optimization, fraud prevention, and geo-targeted feature delivery.

2.5 Sensitive and Financial Data

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, account data or payment account credentials. All payments are securely processed through Stripe, our third-party payment processor. Stripe handles sensitive data per their privacy standards and is solely responsible for PCI compliance.

We only store transaction references, status updates, and metadata necessary for accounting, audit trails, and reward calculations.

2.6 App Store Data Categories

LocalVIP may collect the following data types to operate the Services: Name (such as first and last name), Email Address, a User ID (a unique identifier associated with your account and referral relationships), Precise Location (GPS level location, only if you enable location permissions), and Crash Data (crash logs and diagnostics generated when the app fails). 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

To provide an efficient, responsive, and personalized experience, LocalVIP uses cookies and similar tracking technologies in both our mobile application and web-based services. These tools are essential for maintaining security, analyzing performance, and tailoring interactions based on user behavior. This section explains what technologies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

5.1 What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by our systems or by third-party partners when you use our Services. In addition to cookies, we may also use:

  • Pixel tags (also known as web beacons): These are small graphics that monitor user interactions with content and can measure the effectiveness of email campaigns or user engagement.
  • Local storage and in-app data caching: These methods store information within your device to speed up loading times and maintain session consistency.
  • Device and session identifiers: These help us distinguish repeat visits, track app activity, and detect abnormal use or potential abuse.

These technologies do not give us access to your device, files, or personal content beyond what you explicitly share or permit within the app or browser.

5.2 Why We Use These Technologies

We use cookies and tracking tools for several essential purposes:

  • Performance Optimization: To load pages and app features more quickly and improve responsiveness across device types and network conditions.
  • Functionality: To remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, and support navigation between sections of the app without disruptions.
  • Analytics and Diagnostics: To gather data on how features are used, which screens are most visited, and where users may encounter bugs or slowdowns. This helps us make better design and engineering decisions.
  • Security Monitoring: To detect suspicious patterns, such as repeated failed login attempts or automated scanning of QR codes, helping to protect against fraud or abuse.
  • Location-Dependent Personalization: If location permissions are granted, we may use tracking to offer geographically relevant rewards, notifications, or reminders.

We do not use tracking technologies to collect sensitive personal data or sell information to third parties. All tracking is used for internal operational and service-related purposes only.

5.3 Third-Party Services and Analytics

We may incorporate third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics for Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar tools to help measure usage patterns, campaign effectiveness, and system performance. These tools may use cookies or SDKs within the app to collect usage data in a way that does not identify you personally.

All third-party services used are contractually obligated to adhere to privacy standards aligned with U.S. data protection requirements and must not use your data for unrelated purposes.

5.4 Managing Cookie and Tracking Preferences

As a user, you have the ability to control how and whether tracking technologies are used through the following means:

  • Browser settings: You can adjust cookie settings in your web browser to block or delete cookies. Each browser provides different tools, and changing settings may impact your user experience.
  • Device-level permissions: On mobile devices, you can disable app permissions related to location or data usage through your operating system settings.
  • In-app preferences: Where available, we may offer toggle options within your user profile or privacy settings to adjust notification and tracking behavior.
  • Opt-out mechanisms: For analytics services that support opt-out functionality, we honor industry-standard protocols where applicable.

Note that disabling certain cookies or tracking tools may affect the functionality or personalization capabilities of LocalVIP, including how offers and deals are delivered to you.

5.5 App Tracking Transparency and Tracking

LocalVIP does not track you as Apple defines tracking, meaning we do not link data collected from this app with data collected from other companies’ apps or websites for targeted advertising or advertising measurement, and we do not share user or device data with data brokers for those purposes.

6. Location Data

LocalVIP may request access to your device’s location data in order to deliver a more contextually relevant and personalized experience within our Services. This data helps us enhance user engagement, target local promotions, and present businesses or events that are geographically near you. This section details how and when we collect location information, what we use it for, and how you can control these settings.

6.1 Collection of Location Data

Location data is only collected if you explicitly grant us permission via your device’s operating system. We do not collect real-time GPS information without your consent. Depending on your settings, the types of location data we may collect include:

  • Precise location (e.g., GPS coordinates)
  • Approximate location (e.g., city or region based on network or IP address)
  • Background location data, if explicitly permitted for certain functionality (e.g., continuous proximity alerts or location-aware promotions)

We also log timestamped location events associated with key in-app activities—such as scanning a QR code at a participating business or receiving a nearby offer notification—if location tracking is enabled at that moment.

6.2 How Location Data Is Used

If you grant location permission, LocalVIP uses your precise location to provide core app functionality, including showing nearby businesses offering cashback deals, presenting location relevant offers and campaigns, and supporting location dependent experiences such as in store redemption flows where applicable. We do not collect precise location unless you have enabled location permissions in your device settings, and you can change or revoke permission at any time.

6.3 Third-Party Location Services

We may rely on location services provided by your device’s operating system or third-party SDKs (e.g., Google Location Services or Apple Core Location) to interpret raw location data. These services do not have access to your personal identity within our platform and are subject to their own privacy practices. We require that any third-party tools we integrate comply with data minimization and security obligations.

6.4 User Control and Opt-Out

You are always in control of whether or not LocalVIP can access your location:

  • You may enable or disable location permissions through your device’s app settings at any time.
  • You may adjust your settings to allow only “while using the app” access or to block background tracking entirely.
  • You may still use the app without enabling location data, but some features—like local deals, in-store QR scans, or proximity alerts—may be limited or unavailable.

We respect your choices. No location-based features will be activated unless permission is granted. If at any point you wish to revoke location access or delete any stored location data, please contact us at support@localvip.com, and we will comply promptly.

7. Data Security

At LocalVIP, the privacy and integrity of your data is a core priority. We have implemented a comprehensive security framework to safeguard your personal, business, and behavioral data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration. This section outlines the measures we take to protect your information and what you can do to help secure your account.

7.1 Security Measures We Implement

We apply a multi-layered approach to data protection, combining technical safeguards, administrative procedures, and physical controls to ensure that your information remains secure.

Technical Safeguards include:

  • Encryption of data both at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols such as TLS (Transport Layer Security) and AES-256.
  • Access controls to ensure only authorized personnel or systems can access sensitive information, with role-based permissions enforced across our backend systems.
  • Firewall protection and intrusion detection mechanisms to monitor for suspicious activity or unauthorized access attempts.
  • Regular vulnerability scanning and software updates to ensure our systems are current and resistant to known security exploits.

Administrative Controls include:

  • Mandatory employee training on data privacy and security protocols
  • Internal access audits and logging to track data access or administrative changes
  • Background checks and contractual data security requirements for staff and vendors who may interact with sensitive systems

Physical Security is managed by our infrastructure providers (e.g., cloud data centers), who maintain 24/7 monitoring, biometric access controls, environmental hazard protection, and strict entry protocols for physical locations hosting our servers.

7.2 Stripe and Payment Security

LocalVIP does not process or store your payment card details. All transactions are handled securely through Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor that uses advanced encryption and fraud detection protocols. By using Stripe, we minimize the risk of payment data breaches and shift financial data responsibility to a trusted provider that meets or exceeds industry security benchmarks.

7.3 Your Responsibilities as a User

While we take every reasonable step to protect your data, security is also a shared responsibility. You can help keep your account secure by:

  • Using strong, unique passwords and updating them regularly
  • Avoiding reuse of login credentials across multiple platforms
  • Keeping your device and app updated to receive the latest security patches
  • Not sharing your account details or QR code referrals with untrusted individuals
  • Logging out of public or shared devices when finished using the app

If you suspect unauthorized access or detect suspicious activity associated with your account, you should notify us immediately at support@localvip.com so we can investigate and take appropriate action.

7.4 Breach Notification Protocol

In the unlikely event of a data breach that may compromise your personal information, we will notify affected users without unreasonable delay. This may include in-app messaging, email notification, or other forms of direct communication, in accordance with applicable U.S. state privacy laws and regulatory requirements.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 may access our Services only with the involvement, consent, and supervision of a parent or legal guardian. By using LocalVIP, these users affirm that they have obtained such parental approval.

While we do not proactively collect age-related data for users aged 13 to 17, we encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children’s online activities and to help enforce appropriate privacy and safety measures. We may, at our discretion, remove accounts if we discover they are being used in violation of these guidelines or our Terms of Use.

8.3 Age Verification and Enforcement
